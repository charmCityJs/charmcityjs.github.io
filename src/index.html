Baltimore's JavaScript Community

CharmCityJS brings the web community of Baltimore together on the first Wednesday of every month for an evening of talks, hacking, and networking. Whether you have CTO-level experience or are just getting started with web development, we're excited to meet you.

Talk submissions are always open. Got an idea for a talk or know someone else who does? Awesome! All intakes are handled through GitHub issues.
